abstract: Disinformation on the Internet has been the subject of concern and action
  by multiple actors. While at first the measures were more geared towards training
  and collaborating in the detection of fake news, more and more measures are being
  implemented that involve censorship, blockages, controls and persecution. This article
  critically analyses some of the measures adopted by the State, Internet companies
  and the media in recent years and raises some pending questions in the search for
  solutions.
