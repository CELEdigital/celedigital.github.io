The crime of ‘grooming’ has been included in the Argentinean Criminal Code by means of Law No. 26.904 in 2013, the text of which caused controversy among different sectors. This report analyses the current and projected Argentinean legislation on this crime, both in the Criminal Code regulations and in the text of the Preliminary Draft of the Criminal Code. The aim of the article is to determine whether the proposed draft has been able to overcome the criticisms outlined regarding the inclusion of grooming as a criminal offence. To this end, it analyses both this figure and the procedure that led to its approval, the different bills presented and the criticisms made. Finally, suggestions are made regarding how to advance the debate on the issue.
